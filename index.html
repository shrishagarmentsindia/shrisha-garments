Our Story

Where Comfort
Meets Craft

Founded in 2010, Shrisha Garments started with a single belief — that a great T-shirt should feel like a second skin. We combine premium cotton sourcing with precise craftsmanship to deliver tees worth keeping.

From GSM selection to collar construction, every decision is intentional. No shortcuts. Just pure, wearable quality.

Our Craft

From Cotton to Wardrobe

01
Cotton Sourcing

We source only premium combed and Supima cotton — traceable, breathable, and long-lasting. GSM selected per style for the perfect weight.

02
Precision Cutting

Computer-aided pattern cutting ensures every tee is cut to exact proportions. Minimal waste, maximum accuracy across all sizes.

03
Expert Stitching

Double-stitched seams, reinforced shoulders, and flat-lock hems — our craftsmen sew each garment with precision and pride.

Every single tee passes a 10-point quality inspection — fabric integrity, colour fastness, stitch count, and fit — before it ships.
